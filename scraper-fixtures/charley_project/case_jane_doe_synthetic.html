Details of Disappearance

Doe was last seen leaving her workplace at approximately 5:30pm on the evening of June 13, 1985. She was scheduled to meet a friend for dinner that night but never arrived.

Her vehicle, a silver 1982 Honda Civic, was found three days later at a gas station thirty miles east of her last known location. The driver's-side door was unlocked. Her purse was on the passenger seat with her wallet and identification still inside.

Investigators consider Doe's case suspicious. Anyone with information is asked to contact Claremont PD.
